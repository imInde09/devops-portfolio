# Profile Image Setup

To add your profile image to your portfolio:

1. **Add your photo**: Save your profile image as `profile.jpg` in the `/public` folder
   - Recommended size: 400x400px or higher
   - Recommended format: JPG, PNG, or WebP
   - Recommended aspect ratio: Square (1:1)

2. **Alternative naming**: You can use any name, just update the `src="/profile.jpg"` path in `src/app/page.tsx` line 253

3. **File location**:

```bash
devops-portfolio/
└── public/
    └── profile.jpg
```

The image will automatically display on your portfolio hero section with a nice border and gradient overlay!
